"Nature" refers to the phenomena of the physical world, and also to life in general. It ranges in scale from the subatomic to the cosmic. The term "nature" may refer to living plants and animals, geological processes, weather, and physics, such as matter and energy.The basic nature of life separates animals and plants from inorganic matter, including the ability to grow, reproduce, and die. Learn how to define life, explore its processes, and discover evolution, selection, and mutation.
